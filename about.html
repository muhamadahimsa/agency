Our Team

2021 - 2025

Arunika is a small but passionate team of creatives who care deeply about the work — and the people behind it. We believe good design starts with intention, grows through collaboration, and ends with impact.

Info

Our team is made up of designers, photographers, developers, and creative leads who bring different perspectives to the table, but share one goal: creating work that feels real and thoughtful. We move across disciplines — from branding and ads to digital content and web — always guided by curiosity and a desire to solve problems with clarity and style. With each project, we stay small enough to move fast, and personal enough to care deeply.
